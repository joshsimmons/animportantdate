Colophon

Our design is in the style of airline websites (of course) and the typefaces we chose were inspired by Swissair. Photos are our own and the color palette is based on those, Bootstrap, and palettes found on COLOURlovers.

Open Source

Chris and Josh are both programmers who work specifically with “open source” technology – we use it and do community organizing for like-minded hobbyists and professionals. Open Source Initiative, an organization whose board Josh sits on, created and maintains the open source definition but you might find this explanation more useful.

All that is to say, this website is built using a lot of open source. Our tools, the website itself, the server that runs it, and even the typefaces are almost entirely open source. So, credit where credit is due:

  • Website built with Django*, Python*, Bootstrap*, and Sass*.
  • Fonts include Muli* and Open Sans*.
  • Favicon sourced from EmojiOne*.
  • Tools include Atom*, Git*, and Guetzli*.

Of course, this website itself is open sourced under the MIT license. No, we’re not writing any documentation for it and don’t plan to maintain it 😉
